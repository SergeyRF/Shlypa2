The hat is a fun, team game.

The player needs to explain to his team as many words as possible, guided by the rules of the current round.

Preparing for the game:

1. Add the required number of players to the game.

Enter the player's name, select an avatar for him and click the add button to add it to the game. And you can just generate a random random player.

2. Split players into teams.

The commands are randomly generated. Add commands or remove extra ones until you are satisfied with the result. Players in the team can be mixed.

3. Select game settings.

If you play for the first time, we advise you to set 60 seconds. and 5 words. Random words will be added from the selected dictionary if the player wants. In the original rules of the game, you can not drop words into the hat, but you can (the selected number of points will be deducted).

4. Add words to the hat

Each player must write words for the game or add from the standard set.

The game started!

All words are added to the hat. The round continues as long as there are words in the hat. If the command has guessed the word throw it up and the command will get 1 point. If you skeneh down that word will return to the hat, and the command will take the number of points given in the settings.

Want to see who in which team and the general balls then click the question mark before the move.

Rounds rules:

Round one: Word - Words

It is necessary to explain to your team as many words as possible in the allotted time. You can not use self-similar, similar in sounding words, use foreign words, the translation of which means a conceived word.

Round two: Word - Gestures

Words are explained by gestures ie. pantomime. You can not say anything out loud, use objects, use other players, show on objects.

Round Three: Word - Word

It is necessary to think of the word association and say one word denoting it (not a root). Gestures are prohibited. After the pronunciation of the association, sounds are also prohibited.

Before the beginning of each round, you can read the rules once again by clicking on the icon indicating the round. After each round, team and player points will be displayed.

After three rounds, the winner's team will be determined - the one with the highest number of points.
